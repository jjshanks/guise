# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, as its code of
conduct. The full, authoritative text is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project — contributing code, filing issues, commenting,
or otherwise taking part in the community — you agree to uphold it.

## In short

We want guise to be a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. In practice that means:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Assume good faith; offer and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Do not engage in harassment, personal attacks, or other unwelcome conduct.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and may remove,
edit, or reject contributions and comments that don't align with them, and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior they deem
inappropriate.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project
maintainer at **jjshanks@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy and security will
be respected.

For the full set of enforcement guidelines and the complete standards, see the
linked Contributor Covenant text above.
